About YourFlix

Launched in 2010, YourFix was created to help avid movie watchers locate their local cinemas and keep up to date on newest films. Whether you enjoy watching romcoms, action thrillers or comedies, we have the UK’s largest database of cinemas. SO, you’ll definitely be spoilt for choice.
Nothing beats the cinematic experience, we want to make sure that all Londoners have the chance to catch their favourite movie as they come out.
